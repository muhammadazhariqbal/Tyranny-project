The Book Series

              The first book of this series,FROM A "RACE OF MASTERS" TO A "MASTER RACE": 1948 TO 1848 was released in February of 2013 as an advanced printing. The book tells the 100 year history of the international eugenics movement from the first attempt to breed humans in order to create a "race of masters" until the culmination in The Holocaust as a result of Adolf Hitler's fascination with the creation of a "master race."

              However, it quickly became apparent that there was more than one book to be written with the vast amount of previously unpublished historical evidence still hiding in the archives. Thus, "Race of Masters" was released as an A.R.C. (Advanced Readers Copy) in order to release initial research findings on paperback before formalizing or finalizing and publishing it hardbound.

              The second book of the series, H.H. LAUGHLIN: AMERICAN PROGRESSIVE. AMERICAN SCIENTIST. NAZI COLLABORATOR. will be released in early 2015 also as an A.R.C. (Advanced Readers Copy). The book was made possible when the Carnegie Institution of Washington, D.C. allowed this author to view its internal communications on the Cold Spring Harbor Eugenics Records Office. These files had not been seen since the lab was closed in the midst of The Holocaust. They give an insider's perspective on the scientists that were collaborating with Hitler's government.
